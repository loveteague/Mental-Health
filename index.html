Global Warming

The steady rise in the planet's average temperature is known as global warming.

The burning of fossil fuels has caused this warming trend, which has been going on for quite some time, but has picked up much more speed in the previous century.

The amount of fossil fuels burned has increased along with the human population.

Climate change is a new problem brought on by global warming. Although the terms can occasionally be used interchangeably, they are not the same.

The term "climate change" describes changes to global growth seasons and weather patterns. It also describes the rise in sea level brought on by the melting of glaciers and ice sheets and the expansion of warmer oceans. Climate change, a result of global warming, poses a major threat to life as we know it on Earth.
